1 Peters 5:7

Give God all of your worries, all of your stress, all of your pain, all of your anxiety, he can handle them all.

Joshua 1:9

Have I not commanded you? Be strong and courageous. Do not be frightened, and do not be dismayed for God is with you wherever you go.

2 Timothy 4:17

The Lord will stand with you and give you strength.

Deuteronomy 31:6

Be strong! Be fearless! Don't be afraid and don't be scared by your enemies, because the Lord your god is the one who marches with you. He won't let you down, and he won't abandon you.
